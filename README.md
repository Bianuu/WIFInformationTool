The WiFi Information Tool app is an Android tool designed to analyze and monitor WiFi networks. Through a simple and intuitive interface, users can quickly view detailed information about available networks, signal strength, and internet connection performance. The purpose of the app is to provide a clear picture of the network environment in which the device is located, helping users optimize their wireless connection. The app includes several useful features, such as WiFi signal analysis, router information display (IP address, MAC, network name), and scanning of devices connected via the ARP protocol. In addition, it offers the possibility of performing a speed test to evaluate the performance of the internet connection. By combining these features, WiFi Information Tool becomes a practical aid for anyone who wants to check and improve their WiFi network, offering the perfect balance between simplicity and utility.
